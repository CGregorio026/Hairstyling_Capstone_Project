HairStyling

Hairstyling provides many of the same services as would cosmetology, with the exception to skin and nails. In this program, you will learn the skills necessary for maintaining along with working with clients and sanitation practices. In addition, students will gain knowledge about the state laws and business management skills for an industry. As hair is essential to this program, students will learn to work with all types of hair as well as providing superior client service. Students goals are to pass the Arizona State Board of Cosmetology by the end of the program. This exam consists of hands-on demonstrations and a written test.


    West-Mec requirements:
  • Must be a junior or senior.
  • Two year program, with a Min of 10 high school credits.
  • Two credits of English with a C or better.
  • Proof of residency in West-MEC district boundaries & a copy of birth certificate and immunizations.

    End Result:
  • Certification: Arizona State Board of Cosmetology.
  • Career: Hairstylist, Color Technician, Salon Owner, Salon Manager.
  • Salary range: 10.00 - 15.00 an hour.
