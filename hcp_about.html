HairStyling
Hairstyling provides many of the same services as would cosmetologist,

with the exception to skin and nails. In this program, you will learn the

skills necessary for maintaining along with working with clients and

sanitation practices. In addition, students will gain knowledge about

the state laws and business management skills for an industry. Hair is

essential to this program so, students will learn to work with all types of

hair as well as providing superior client service. Students goals are to

pass the Arizona State board of Cosmetology by the end of the

program. This exam consist of hands on demonstrations and written

test.



West-Mec :

Must be a junior or senior.

Two year program, with a Min of 10 high school credits.

Two credits of English with a C or better.

Proof of residency in West-MEC district boundaries & a copy of birth

certificate and immunizations.



Certification: Arizona State board of Cosmetology.

Career: hair stylist, color technician, salon owner, salon manager.

Salary range: 10.00 - 15.00 an hour.
